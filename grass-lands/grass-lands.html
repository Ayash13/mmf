Grass Lands

About

The first curated cannabis experience at a major American music festival returns for year four! Grass Lands is a 21+ celebration of all things cannabis, with education and resources about how it integrates into our daily lives.

Head to SoPo (South of the Polo Fields) and explore the Grass Lands town square for all the essentials: buy goods from some of the finest purveyors around at The Farmers’ Market by High Times, including Grass Lands-exclusive products from Timeless, Woodstock Heritage and Sundae School; consume your purchases at the Garden of Weed’n; stop by delicious food vendors when cravings hit, and take in some live music in the Arts District. On Friday Grass Lands will be treated to music from DJ Funklor, Saturday DJ Sep will be bringing the good vibes, and Sunday will be closed out by APE - a six-piece band that carves tikis during their set!

Want to skip the lines at The Farmers’ Market by High Times? Download the Outside Lands app and pre-order your products ahead of time. They’ll be ready to pick up when you arrive!
